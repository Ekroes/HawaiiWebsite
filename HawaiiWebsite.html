    • Hawaii is one of the most beautiful states in the United States of America. Created by a very active volcanic hotspot,
    the resulting shield volcanos are the basis for the amazing islands we visit. The culture is a wonderful mix of native
    Polynesian, Japanese and American traditions.

    Though near the equator the island of Hawaii still managages to hosta wide variety of ecosystems,
    from tropical beaches to arctic tundra on the mountain tops. This diverse environment allows a
    great number of unique plants and animals to thrive on the island.
